# PaperProof Whitepaper

This directory is for the PaperProof whitepaper.

## Purpose

The whitepaper should communicate the vision, ecosystem role, user value,
governance direction, token relevance, and long-term roadmap of PaperProof. It
should be more accessible than the yellow paper and less formal than the
academic paper.

## Target Audience

- Community members.
- Potential users and artifact publishers.
- Sui and Walrus ecosystem participants.
- Developers deciding whether to build on PaperProof.
- Potential partners, supporters, and funders.

## Suggested Files

- `paperproof-whitepaper.tex`
- `refs.bib`
- `figures/`
- `tokenomics/` or appendix material, if needed later.

## Suggested Structure

1. Executive Summary
2. Why Durable Artifact Infrastructure Matters
3. What PaperProof Is
4. Core Use Cases
5. Protocol Roles
6. Sui and Walrus Fit
7. Artifact Types and Lifecycle
8. Community Interaction and Governance
9. PPRF Token Role
10. SDK and Developer Ecosystem
11. Agentic and Indexer Future
12. Roadmap
13. Risk, Safety, and Disclaimers
14. Conclusion

## Notes

The whitepaper should make PaperProof understandable and credible without
requiring readers to inspect Move source code. Avoid overpromising. Emphasize
what is already deployed, what is experimental, and what depends on future
community adoption.
